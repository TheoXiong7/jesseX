Social Work

Making a difference beyond the ice

Youth Hockey Programs

Empowering the next generation

I volunteer as a mentor and coach for underprivileged youth in our community, teaching them not just hockey skills but life values like teamwork, perseverance, and leadership. Through free hockey clinics and equipment donations, we're making the sport accessible to everyone, regardless of their economic background.

Educational Support

Building futures through learning

Education is the key to breaking cycles of poverty. I work with local schools to provide tutoring services and scholarship opportunities for student-athletes. Our program has helped over 50 students improve their grades while pursuing their athletic dreams, showing them that they don't have to choose between sports and academics.

Mental Health Awareness

Breaking the stigma, one conversation at a time

Mental health in sports is often overlooked. I advocate for open conversations about mental wellness, sharing my own experiences with performance anxiety and the pressures of competitive sports. Through workshops and speaking engagements, I help athletes understand that seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.

Community Outreach

Giving back to those who supported me

Regular volunteer work at local food banks, homeless shelters, and senior centers keeps me grounded and connected to my community. I organize annual charity drives during the hockey season, raising funds and awareness for various causes. It's important to remember that success is meaningless unless it's shared with others.
